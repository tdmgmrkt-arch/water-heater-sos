14. UV Air Purifier (consider rolling under Air Filtration if needing to trim to 13 pages)

### City Page Strategy
For top 5 services (AC Repair, AC Install, Furnace Repair, Mini Split Install, Heat Pump Install) — create city variants for the **9 high-population cities** with strongest map-pack opportunity:
- Riverside, Corona, Moreno Valley, San Bernardino, Fontana, Rancho Cucamonga, Ontario, Temecula, Murrieta

For tier-2 cities (Redlands, Hemet, Menifee, Lake Elsinore, Eastvale, Chino, Chino Hills, Upland, Yucaipa, Rialto, Colton), use a single "service area" page per main service that links to/from main service pages.

### SERP Feature Plays
- **AI Overview wins:** Heat Pump, Whole House Humidifier/Dehumidifier, HVAC Maintenance Plan, IAQ pages — write Q&A-style content with clear schema (FAQPage, HowTo where relevant).
- **Featured Snippet wins:** Thermostat Installation, HVAC Maintenance Plan, Humidifier/Dehumidifier — target 40-60 word definitions at top of page.
- **Map Pack wins:** AC Repair, Furnace Repair, AC Install, Mini Split, AC Maintenance — invest in GBP + reviews + city landing pages.
